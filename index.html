How it works

Get started in a few steps: add friends, choose who can see your location, and start sharing.

1

Add friends

Invite people you trust. FriendMap uses your phone number so you connect with real contacts.

2

Share your location

Turn on location sharing when you want. You decide who sees you and when.

3

See friends on the map

View friends’ locations in real time on the map and get directions to meet up.

4

Convoys & events

Drive together in convoys with turn-by-turn, or create events and invite friends to join.

Features

Real-time location sharing, convoys, events, AR tagging, and built-in safety – all in one app.

Real-time location sharing

Share your location with selected friends. See each other on the map and navigate to meet.

Convoy mode

Drive in a group with live positions and optional push-to-talk so everyone stays in sync.

Events

Create events, set a place and time, invite friends, and track who’s on the way.

AR tagging

Drop AR tags in the real world for friends to find – great for meet-up spots and discoveries.

SOS & emergency

One-tap SOS: in-app alarm, call 911, call your trusted contact, or send your live location by SMS. Minors use parent as trusted contact.

Age verification & safety for teens

Users 15 and under need parent or guardian verification (including ID) before using the app. We keep teens safe without blocking the experience.
